**LED** — glossy indicator light from the Pokédex shell. Big blue one is the lens; small red/yellow/green are status lights.

```jsx
<LED color="blue" size={48} />
<LED color="red" size={12} /> <LED color="yellow" size={12} /> <LED color="green" size={12} />
```

Props: `color`, `size`, `lit`, `glow`.
